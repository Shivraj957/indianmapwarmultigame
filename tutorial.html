Indian Map War Multigame

Tutorial

1. Enter your username, select your region and choose your game mode. The game mode is either ludo or uno. 2. This game is played in 2 teams. The teams are chosen randomly. 3. A player is paired with a random player from the opposing team. 4. If the game mode is ludo, all the players in the same room also play ludo. The same applies for uno.
